Privacy Policy

Last updated:

No account No ads Kid-friendly
Lumo Kids: Play and Learn is designed for young children. We aim to collect as little data as possible.

Overview

This Privacy Policy explains how Lumo Kids: Play and Learn ("the App") handles information. The App is intended for children and does not require creating an account.

Information We Collect

We do not collect personally identifiable information such as names, emails, phone numbers, or precise location.

Analytics

If we enable analytics in the future, we will only use it to understand app performance (e.g., crashes, basic usage) and to improve the experience. We will not use analytics for advertising or tracking across apps.

In-App Purchases

The App may offer one-time purchases. Purchases are processed by Apple. We do not receive your payment details (such as card information).

Children’s Privacy

The App is designed for kids and follows a minimal-data approach. We do not request personal data from children. If you believe we may have collected personal information by mistake, please contact us and we will delete it.

Third-Party Services

The App may rely on Apple services (e.g., App Store, In-App Purchases). These services may collect information as described in Apple’s policies.

Data Security

We take reasonable steps to protect the App and any limited data that may exist (e.g., technical logs).

Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at: support@lumo.kids
