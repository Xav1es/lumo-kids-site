Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-02-11

Lumo Kids: Play and Learn is designed for young children and is intended to be safe and family-friendly.

This app does not require an account and does not ask children to enter personal information.

Information We Collect

We do not collect personally identifiable information such as name, address, phone number, or email inside the app.

Analytics

If you enable analytics tools (e.g., Firebase Analytics / AppsFlyer), they may collect limited technical data such as device information, app version, and general usage events to help improve the app.

This data is used for app performance and product improvement and is not used to identify a child.

Purchases

The app may offer one-time in-app purchases (non-consumable unlocks). Purchases are processed by Apple. We do not receive your payment details.

Advertising

Lumo Kids does not show third-party ads.

Data Sharing

We do not sell personal data. If analytics are used, data may be processed by the analytics provider under their terms.

Children’s Privacy

This app is intended for families and young children. Parents/guardians should supervise children’s use of devices and purchases.

Contact

If you have questions, contact us at: support@lumo.kids
